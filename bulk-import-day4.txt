Q: What does the 'O' in OODA stand for?
A. Observe
B. Orient
C. Operate
D. Organise
Correct: A
Explanation: OODA stands for Observe, Orient, Decide, Act - a decision cycle developed for combat operations that applies to tactical driving.

Q: What does the 'D' in OODA stand for?
A. Detect
B. Decide
C. Defend
D. Direct
Correct: B
Explanation: The D in OODA stands for Decide - making a rapid decision based on observed information and orientation.

Q: What is the primary purpose of the OODA loop in tactical driving?
A. To maintain vehicle speed
B. To process information and act faster than the threat
C. To communicate with convoy members
D. To navigate unfamiliar routes
Correct: B
Explanation: The OODA loop's purpose is to cycle through observe-orient-decide-act faster than adversaries, gaining a tactical advantage.

Q: What is a 'threat' in the Risk vs Threat framework?
A. General possibility of harm
B. Specific and identified danger

D. Environmental condition
Correct: B
Explanation: A threat is a specific and identified danger with intent and capability to cause harm, as opposed to general risk.
